Summary

Analysis of Medicare/Medicaid calue of care in the US.

Our analysis explores the relationship between revenues generated by hospitals via Medicare/Medicaid programs.

The Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services provides a robust dataset outlining cost of care metrics across four major care categories broken down by zipcode served. We examined the cost of care data versus median household income from the US Census Bureau.

We observed total revenues starkly increase in urban areas and areas with low median house incomes.
